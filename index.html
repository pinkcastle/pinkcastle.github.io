Radical new futures requiure radical new aesthetics


How do we maintain hope in a world saturated with myopic individualism, attention economics and a disconnection with nature? What does an alternative world even look like? Speculative design gives us a mechanism to render those futures clear. Through graphic and furniture design, film and geodesics, we explore a new aesthetic for solarpunk futures.

Geodesics


Geodesic domes offer dynamic new spaces to cultivate a more human future. Perfect for immersive 3D visuals, greenhousing, or intimacy, their geometry speaks to a new way of life.

Life on Mars

An interpretive music video of Seu Jorge’s variant of the Bowie classic, “Life on Mars” presents a perspective on social media within our world. As our lead character bikes around the funky, artistic wilderness of Mars Beach, he remains absorbed in the scroll, missing the real encounter in exchange for its filtered, mediated counterpart. That is, until an obstacle quite literally becomes caught in the wheel of the social machine, forcing our protagonist to divert from his screen and the audience to inquire whether Mars is more than just a physical place.
